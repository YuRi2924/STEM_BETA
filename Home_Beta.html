Dive in into world of STEM.

STEM is an interdisciplinary approach in the Philippine education system that stands for:

SCIENCE

SCIENCE

Science is the systematic study of the structure and behavior of the physical and natural world through observation and experiment.

TECHNOLOGHY

TECHNOLOGHY

Technology is the application of scientific knowledge for practical purposes, especially in industry.

ENGINEERING

ENGINEERING

Engineering is the application of scientific and mathematical principles to design, build, and maintain structures, machines, and systems.

MATHEMATICS

MATHEMATICS

Mathematics is the study of numbers, quantities, shapes, and patterns, and the logical reasoning behind them.
