STEM SOC COMMUNITY

The Community feature offers previews of upcoming STEM Society events. It also highlights activities from other organizations at the University of the Cordilleras. This ensures students stay informed about the latest events and opportunities within the university community. Engaging with these events fosters a collaborative and enriching academic experience.

Visit us in our Facebook page.

Check our community organization.

STEM is an interdisciplinary approach in the Philippine education system that stands for:

STEM SOC

STEM SOCIETY

The STEM Society is a organization dedicated to supporting STEM students by addressing their concerns, and hosting events for STEM students.

NJG

Numerical Jaguar Guild

The Numerical Jaguar Guild is a club that explores the fascinating world of mathematics through engaging activities and collaborative learning.

RYC

Red Cross Youth Council

The Red Cross Youth Council is an organization where people train on how to save one's life and be prepared for different disasters.

AUG

Artificial Universe Guild

The Artificial Universe Guild is a club dedicated to exploring the realms of technology and physics.
